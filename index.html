About Our Product

  • Our all-natural plucking fingers are a sustainable alternative to rubber, made from renewable banana fibers. This product removes feathers effectively while reducing environmental impact and chemical use. Durable and non-toxic, it’s ideal for poultry shops, distributors, and processing facilities. Our innovative feather-plucking machine uses banana plant material instead of rubber, offering a hygienic and efficient solution for small to medium-scale operations.
  • Our product is a natural poultry cleaning aid made from processed banana pseudostem. Designed to remove feathers from chickens without the use of chemicals or fire, this method ensures the skin remains intact and safe for consumption. It’s an eco-friendly alternative that supports healthy living and sustainable cooking.
